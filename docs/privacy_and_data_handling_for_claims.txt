PRIVACY & DATA HANDLING (INTERNAL SUMMARY)

Data collected during claims:
- Personal details (name, contact)
- Incident details (location, time, description)
- Vehicle details (registration, photos, repair invoices)
- Third-party details (if provided)
- Police reports (if applicable)

Data usage:
- Claims handling and investigation
- Fraud prevention and risk analysis
- Customer support and communications

Retention:
- Stored for required legal/operational periods.
- Access limited to authorized staff and systems.

Customer guidance:
- Share only relevant information.
- Keep sensitive documents secure.
- Ask for clarification on what is required and why.

This is a simplified internal summary for prototype behavior and scripting.
